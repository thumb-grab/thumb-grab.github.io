How to Download Thumbnails on iOS Devices

Follow these methods to download YouTube thumbnails on iPhone or iPad:

Method 1: Using Safari with YTDFree.Online

YTDFree.Online is the simplest way to download thumbnails on iOS:

  • Open Safari and visit YTDFree.Online.
  • Copy the YouTube video URL (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc123`) from the YouTube app or browser.
  • Paste the URL into the input field on YTDFree.Online.
  • Select your preferred resolution (e.g., HD 1280x720).
  • Tap “Download.” The thumbnail will save to your Photos app or Files (depending on iOS settings).

Method 2: Manual URL Method

If you prefer a manual approach:

  • Copy the YouTube video ID (e.g., `abc123` from `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc123`).
  • In Safari, enter the URL: `https://img.youtube.com/vi/abc123/maxresdefault.jpg`.
  • Long-press the image and select “Save Image” to store it in Photos.
