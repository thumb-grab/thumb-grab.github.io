Key Colors and Their Psychological Effects

Different colors trigger specific emotions:

  • Red: Urgency, excitement. Ideal for action-oriented videos (e.g., tutorials).
  • Yellow: Optimism, attention-grabbing. Great for lifestyle content.
  • Blue: Trust, calmness. Suits tech or educational videos.
  • Green: Growth, relaxation. Perfect for health or nature content.
  • Purple: Creativity, luxury. Works for art or premium brands.

Combine colors strategically to align with your video’s tone and audience.
