Legal Considerations for Using Thumbnails

Thumbnails are often copyrighted. To use them legally on social media:

  • Get Permission: Contact the video owner for commercial use.
  • Attribute: Credit the source in your post (e.g., “Thumbnail from [YouTube Channel]”).
  • Edit Heavily: Modify thumbnails to avoid copyright flags.
